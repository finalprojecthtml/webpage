Wolf Description

Wolves are the largest and most successful canine species, and are often seen as a symbol of the wilderness.

Interesting Facts about Wolves

  • Wolves are social animals and live in packs, typically consisting of 10-15 individuals, including pups, adults, and older wolves.
  • Wolves are the only large mammal species that has successfully domesticated.
  • The gray wolf has a population of approximately 250,000 to 350,000 individuals.
